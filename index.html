YAP - Roommate Connection App

A mobile application designed by university students to enhance communication and strengthen relationships through interactive, gamified experiences.

Scope

Interaction design, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing for a mobile roommate connection application.

Tools

Figma, Adobe XD, Miro, Notion

Background

For this project, we were tasked with identifying a unique challenge that could be addressed through the development of an app. YAP was created to improve roommate relationships by facilitating meaningful conversations and engagement through interactive features.

Problem Statement

Many university students face difficulties in communicating and forming meaningful relationships with their roommates. YAP addresses this issue by providing structured, engaging activities that promote deeper connections.

Design Process

Our design process began with research and low-fidelity prototyping. Following usability testing in Milestone 2, we identified key areas for improvement, including enhanced search filters for discussion-based games and the integration of an Invite Friends feature to encourage broader participation.

Solution

YAP offers key features designed to foster better roommate relationships:

  • Invite Friends: Users can add roommates, friends, or groups to interactive activities.
  • Daily Question Prompt: Thought-provoking questions help spark meaningful discussions.
  • Gamified Interactions: Fun and engaging activities encourage communication and bonding.
  • Shared Memories: Users can upload and share images, quotes, and significant moments to strengthen their connections.
  • Insights & Analytics: Personalized feedback provides insights into communication patterns and ways to improve interactions.
